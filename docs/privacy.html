Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

RefineKeyboard ("we", "our", or "the app") is a custom iOS keyboard that uses artificial intelligence to help you rewrite and refine text. This policy explains what data we handle, how it is used, and what we never do.

1. What We Process

RefineKeyboard only processes text when you explicitly tap one of the AI action buttons (Refine, Warm, Professional, or Short). At that moment, the text currently in your message field is sent to our server for processing.

  • We do not read, log, or transmit your keystrokes passively.
  • We do not collect anything you type unless you tap an AI button.
  • Text sent for processing is not stored on our servers after the refined result is returned to your device.

2. How Your Text Is Processed

When you tap an AI button, the selected text is sent over an encrypted HTTPS connection to our backend server, which forwards it to OpenAI's API for rewriting. The refined text is returned to your device and inserted into your message field. Neither we nor OpenAI retain the content after the response is delivered.

OpenAI's own privacy practices are described at openai.com/policies/privacy-policy.

3. Full Access

iOS requires "Allow Full Access" to be enabled for a keyboard extension to make network requests. We request Full Access solely to enable the AI rewriting feature. We do not use Full Access to collect any additional data beyond what is described in Section 1.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID account settings.

5. Data We Store

The only preference data stored on your device is:

  • Your selected output language preference.
  • Your subscription status (stored locally so the keyboard knows whether AI features are unlocked).
  • An optional custom API endpoint for developer use.

None of this is transmitted to us or to any third party.

6. Children's Privacy

RefineKeyboard is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you within the app.
